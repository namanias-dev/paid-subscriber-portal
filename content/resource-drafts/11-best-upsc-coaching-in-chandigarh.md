<h2>Questions to Ask on Your Demo Class</h2>
<ol>
<li>Who will actually teach my batch, and how accessible are they for doubts?</li>
<li>How often are Mains answers evaluated, and who evaluates them?</li>
<li>What does the test series cover, and is there post-test discussion?</li>
<li>What is the batch size?</li>
<li>What exactly is included in the fee (foundation, optional, test series, materials)?</li>
<li>What guidance is there for optional subject selection and preparation?</li>
<li>Are there online/hybrid options if I can't always attend in person?</li>
</ol>

<h2>Red Flags to Avoid</h2>
<ul>
<li><strong>Vague, unverifiable result claims</strong> ("thousands of selections") with no specifics.</li>
<li><strong>Huge batches</strong> where you'll never get personal attention.</li>
<li><strong>No answer evaluation</strong> or only token feedback.</li>
<li><strong>Hidden fees</strong> revealed after you enrol.</li>
<li><strong>Pressure to pay immediately</strong> without a genuine demo.</li>
</ul>
