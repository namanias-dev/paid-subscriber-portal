# Dashboard

**Menu:** `Overview` → `Dashboard`  ·  **Web address:** `/admin`
**Who can open it:** any logged-in admin (revenue figures are hidden unless your role has **View revenue**).

## What this page is for
A quick "academy at a glance" home screen. Heading: `Dashboard`, subtitle `Your academy at a glance`. It's read-only — there are no buttons to change anything.

## The KPI cards
- `Total Leads`
- `Total Students`
- `Active Subs` (active subscriptions)
- `Conversion`
- `Revenue (Month)` and `Revenue (Total)` — only visible with **View revenue**
- `Pending Collections` — only with **View revenue**
- `Webinar Regs`

## Duplicate-enrollment alert (Super Admin only)
If any student has booked the **same course more than once**, a red banner appears at the top of the dashboard with a count and a **Review & merge →** button. It links to the **Duplicate Enrollments** tool where you keep one enrollment and cancel the rest. The banner clears automatically once everything is merged. See **[Duplicate Enrollments](duplicate-enrollments)**.

## The charts
- `Enrollments over time`
- `Revenue by course`
- `Lead source breakdown`
- `Funnel: Lead → Admission`

## Where the data comes from
It combines leads, students, payments, webinars, enrollments and courses behind the scenes. Use it for a daily pulse; use the specific pages (Leads, Payments, Students, Analytics) for detail and actions.
