# Getting Started (Read Me First)

Welcome! This is the staff guide for the Naman Sharma IAS Academy **Admin Panel**. It is written in plain English for non-technical team members. You do **not** need to know anything technical to use it.

## What is the Admin Panel?

It is the private control center for the academy. From here staff can see leads, students, payments, webinars, send SMS, manage content, and more. The public website (what students see) is separate — in the Admin Panel you will sometimes see a `View site ↗` link at the top right to open the public site.

## How to log in

1. Open the admin web address in your browser.
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password** (given to you by an administrator).
3. Click to sign in.
4. The first time, you may be asked: `For security, please change your temporary password.` Click `Change password` and set your own.

If you forget your password, ask a Super Admin to reset it for you on the **Staff & Roles** page.

## The screen layout

- **Left sidebar** — the menu. Items are grouped (for example `Overview`, `Sales`, `Academics`, `People`). Click any item to open that page.
- **Top bar** — shows the page, your username, your **role** (a coloured pill like `Super Admin`), a `Password` button, and `View site ↗`.
- **Main area** — the page you opened.
- **Logout** — the `↩ Logout` button at the bottom of the left sidebar.
- On a **phone**, the sidebar is hidden behind the `☰` button at the top left.

## ❓ Help & Learn button

On every admin page there is a **`❓ Help & Learn`** button (top bar / floating). Click it to open this handbook. It automatically shows the guide for the page you are on, lets you **search** all topics, browse the full **topic list**, and **ask a question**. You can open it any time you are stuck.

## Why some menu items or buttons are missing for you

The Admin Panel uses **roles and permissions**. Your role decides what you can see and do. If a menu item or button does not appear for you, it usually means **your role does not have that permission** — this is normal and intended, not a bug.

- Example: only staff with the **revenue** permission see `Payments & Finance`.
- Example: only staff with the **send SMS** permission see `SMS Mission Control`.
- A **Super Admin** sees and can do everything.

See the **Roles & Permissions** guide for the full list of who can do what. If you believe you need access to something, ask a Super Admin to adjust your role.

## A few golden rules

- ⚠️ **Money and access actions are real.** Recording a payment, accepting a proof, granting or revoking access, and sending SMS all take effect immediately and affect real students. Double-check names and amounts.
- ⚠️ **Some actions cannot be undone** (for example deleting a webinar deletes its registrations too). Look for the “⚠️ Be careful” notes in each guide.
- When in doubt, open `❓ Help & Learn` and read the recipe, or ask a Super Admin.
